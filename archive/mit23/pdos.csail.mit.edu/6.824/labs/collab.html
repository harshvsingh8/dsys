Collaboration Policy

You must write all the code you hand in for 6.5840, except for code that we give you as part of assignments. You are not allowed to look at anyone else's solution, and you are not allowed to look at solutions from previous 6.5840 or 6.824 years. You may discuss the assignments with other students, but you may not look at or copy each others' code. The reason for this rule is that we believe you will learn the most by designing and implementing your lab solution yourself.

Please do not publish your code or make it available to current or future 6.5840 students. github.com repositories are public by default, so please don't put your code there unless you make the repository private. You may find it convenient to use MIT's GitHub, but be sure to create a private repository.
